Job Title: Backend Developer (Python)

Responsibilities:
- Develop and maintain backend services and REST APIs
- Collaborate with frontend developers and product managers

- Familiarity with relational databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL)
- Good understanding of software development practices
- Experience with Docker and version control (Git) is a plus

Nice to have:
- Exposure to CI/CD pipelines and cloud platforms
